Describe what is VLAN and how it works?
//VLAN mean Virtual Local Area Network and it is a logical group of devices. It is a group of devices which are connected to the same switch. VLANs are used to separate the broadcast domains. VLANs are created using VTP protocol.
What is the difference between VLAN and VTP?
//VLAN is a logical group of devices and VTP is a protocol which is used to create and manage VLANs.
What do we mean by desirable and auto ports?
//Desirable ports are those ports which are configured as trunk ports and auto ports are those ports which are configured as access ports.
